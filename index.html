Example Recipe: Quick and Easy Tomato and Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes to the saucepan and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  4. Stir in the chopped basil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot. Pour the tomato sauce over the pasta and stir to combine.
  6. Serve hot with additional basil and freshly grated parmesan cheese, if desired.
